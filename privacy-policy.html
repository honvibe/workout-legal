Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 29, 2026

Home Workout - 7 Min Exercise ("the App") is developed by Nattapon Kittisuphat ("we", "us"). This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of data:

  • Usage Data: Workout activity, app interactions, exercise preferences, and streak data — stored locally on your device.
  • Analytics Data: Anonymous usage events (app opens, workout completions, feature usage) sent to Mixpanel for product improvement.
  • Advertising Data: Device advertising identifier (IDFA) used by Google AdMob to serve relevant ads, subject to your App Tracking Transparency (ATT) consent.
  • Purchase Data: In-app purchase and subscription records managed by Apple — we do not collect or store payment information.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and improve the App's workout features
  • To track your fitness progress and streaks
  • To display relevant advertisements (free users only)
  • To analyze app performance and user behavior anonymously
  • To send workout reminders (with your permission)

3. Third-Party Services

The App uses the following third-party services:

  • Google AdMob — Advertising. Google Privacy Policy
  • Mixpanel — Analytics. Mixpanel Privacy Policy
  • Apple StoreKit — In-app purchases and subscriptions.

4. Advertising & Tracking

We use Google AdMob to display ads to free users. Before collecting your IDFA for personalized advertising, we request your consent via Apple's App Tracking Transparency (ATT) framework. You can change this at any time in your device Settings.

Pro Classic and Pro Premium users do not see any advertisements.

5. Data Storage & Security

Your workout data (exercises, streaks, preferences) is stored locally on your device using UserDefaults. We do not maintain user accounts or store personal data on external servers.

Analytics data sent to Mixpanel is anonymous and cannot be used to identify you personally.

6. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

7. Your Rights

You can:

  • Opt out of ad tracking via Settings → Privacy → Tracking
  • Delete all local app data by uninstalling the App
  • Request information about your data by contacting us

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.
